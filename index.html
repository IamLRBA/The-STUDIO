Inclusivity and Accessibility

FusionCraft STUDIOS is committed to being an inclusive community where everyone is welcome, regardless of age, ethnicity, gender, sexuality, income, or religion. We strive to create an environment rooted in mutual respect, tolerance, and empowerment.

We actively work towards improving accessibility and inclusivity within our spaces. Our initiatives include collaborating with organizations to ensure our studios are welcoming to individuals with diverse needs, including those who are Deaf, blind, or visually impaired.

While we aim to make our spaces accessible, we acknowledge some limitations. Certain areas may currently pose challenges, such as stairways at entrances and the lack of comprehensive guidance systems for individuals with visual impairments. We continue to explore ways to address these barriers and enhance accessibility for all.

What Does It Cost?

FusionCraft STUDIOS is primarily funded through memberships. While membership is not required to use our space or host events, we encourage it for those who visit frequently. The standard membership rate is $30 per month, with reduced rates available for students and individuals facing financial challenges.

Many of our tools and facilities are free to use, but contributions for materials and maintenance are greatly appreciated. Some specialized equipment, like the laser cutter, incurs a usage fee of $0.60 per minute to cover operational costs.
